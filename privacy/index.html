Privacy Policy

How Speedy SlideShow 25 handles your data — straightforward and honest.

Last updated · April 2026

Overview

Speedy SlideShow 25 is a Windows desktop application developed by BA Commander Limited. This privacy policy explains how the application handles your data.

Data Collection

Speedy SlideShow 25 does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The application runs entirely on your local machine. No information about you, your photos, or your usage is ever sent to any server or third party.

Photos and Files

All photos and files processed by Speedy SlideShow 25 remain on your device at all times. The application reads files from folders you select and does not upload, copy, or transmit them anywhere.

Internet Access

Speedy SlideShow 25 may use an internet connection solely to look up location names for photos that contain GPS coordinates (reverse geocoding). No photo data, personal information, or identifying information is transmitted during this process.
